Who We Are

Durga Car Keys is a leading provider of professional automotive locksmith services in Vadodara, Gujarat. With years of experience in the industry, we specialize in car and bike key duplication, programming, and emergency lockout assistance for all makes and models.

Founded by Bharat Rajout, our business is built on the principles of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We use the latest technology and equipment to ensure fast, accurate, and secure key services for our valued customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and professional car key services to vehicle owners across Vadodara and surrounding areas. We strive to be the most trusted name in automotive locksmith services by:

  • Providing 24/7 emergency assistance when you need it most
  • Building lasting relationships with our customers through trust and reliability
